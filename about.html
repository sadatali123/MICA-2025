About MICA 2025

MICA annual conference series has evolved into a significant multidisciplinary platform that brings together academics, scientists, industry researchers, scholars, and students in the fields of advanced Communications and Machine Intelligence. The conference serves as a platform for professionals, researchers, and industry experts to exchange knowledge, share cutting-edge research findings, and foster collaboration. The conference will promote industry engagement, disseminate research outcomes to wider audiences, and contribute to professional development through tutorials and keynote presentations. By bringing together diverse stakeholders, MICA 2025 will play a pivotal role in building communities, driving innovation, and advancing the collective understanding of machine intelligence and advanced communication technologies.

Proceedings of MICA 2025 will be published by Springer (proposed)


Indexing: The series will be submitted for inclusion to the leading indexing services including ISI Proceedings, EI-Compendex, DBLP, SCOPUS, Google Scholar and Springerlink.


About ICFAI University, Tripura

Located in the peaceful and green state of Tripura, ICFAI University Tripura is a rising hub of modern education, innovation, and student growth. Since its establishment in 2004, the university has steadily built a strong reputation or offering quality education that blends academic knowledge with practical skills.The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Law, Science, Arts, Education, and Special Education. Among these, the Faculty of Science and Technology (FST) is particularly dynamic, with departments in Computer Science, AI & ML, Data Science, Electronics, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering. The programs are designed to meet current industry demands and prepare students for real-world challenges.What truly sets ICFAI University Tripura apart is its student-focused environment and commitment to holistic education. The campus encourages learning not just inside classrooms but through hands-on projects, workshops, internships, and frequent interactions with industry professionals. Faculty members are approachable, experienced, and deeply involved in guiding students academically and professionally. The university is also steadily growing as a center for research and innovation, with students and faculty working on projects in cutting-edge fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and sustainable technologies. The learning environment here promotes curiosity, creativity, and collaboration.ICFAI University Tripura is more than just an educational institution – it’s a place where young minds grow into confident professionals, thinkers, and responsible citizens.


About the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, ICFAI University, Tripura:

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at ICFAI University, Tripura, is a dynamic center of learning and innovation, dedicated to shaping the next generation of computing professionals and researchers. Known for its academic rigor and industry-aligned curriculum, the department offers a wide range of programs including B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), M.Tech in CSE, and Ph.D. in Engineering and Technology. With a strong emphasis on both foundational theory and practical application, the department equips students with in-demand skills in areas such as data science, machine learning, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and full-stack development. The curriculum is frequently updated to match current technological trends and industry needs, ensuring graduates are ready to thrive in competitive environments.The department is supported by a dedicated team of faculty members who bring a mix of academic expertise and real-world experience. Many are actively involved in research, consultancy, and scholarly publications, contributing to the advancement of computer science in both academia and industry. Students benefit from hands-on exposure through well-equipped laboratories, project-based learning, coding competitions, hackathons, and collaborative research.The department also encourages strong industry linkages, facilitating internships, expert talks, workshops, and placement opportunities with top companies across India. This industry-academic integration ensures that students are not only academically strong but also professionally prepared.

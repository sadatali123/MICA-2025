Call for Reviewers

Proposals for reviewing articles of MICA 2025 are invited from academicians and industry experts from highly-reputed organizations. The proposals should clearly indicate:

a. Name and affiliation of the person

b. Area of research

Reviewing proposals along with a short curriculum vitae (including publications) of the person can be mailed to micaconf@gmail.com. After review by an external expert panel, decision will be intimated within 7 working days from submission of the proposal.

*Notes:
a. Names of all reviewers will be published in Springer proceedings.
b. The conference chairs will forward reviewing certificate to all reviewers.
